Digital Artist & VFX Professional

Biography

Loucas Rongeart is a dedicated digital artist and VFX professional with a passion for creature design, concept art, and visual storytelling.

With extensive experience in The Foundry's Nuke, Loucas specializes in compositing, visual effects, and digital workflows that bring creative visions to life.

His work spans multiple disciplines including concept art, creature design, digital sculpting, and environmental art, with a focus on creating immersive and atmospheric pieces that captivate audiences.

Through his Notion Courses platform, Loucas shares his expertise with aspiring artists and professionals, offering comprehensive tutorials and insights into modern digital art workflows.

Specializations:

  • Visual Effects Compositing (Nuke)
  • Creature Design & Character Development
  • Digital Concept Art
  • 3D Digital Sculpting
  • Environmental & Atmospheric Art
  • Educational Content Creation
