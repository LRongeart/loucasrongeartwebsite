About Me

Creature & Character Artist

Loucas Rongeart

I'm primarily a Character / Creature artist, mostly specialized in Texturing & LookDevelopment for Hero Assets for VFX & Feature Film projects.

Proficient with most proprietary & off-the-shelf softwares, with a bias for resilient production products such as Mari, Nuke, Maya, Houdini, ZBrush & Katana.

Always expanding my horizons & remaining curious are my uttermost goals.

For the past 10 years, sharing my passion & know-hows with colleagues and students in broadly different production environments made me a better team-player, produce documented creative processes, and work on my communication skill sets.
